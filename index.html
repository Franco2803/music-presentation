My Music Taste:
Before vs Now

This presentation is about how my music taste changed over the years. From gaming music and hype songs during quarantine to more emotional and personal music today.

  • What I used to listen to
  • The artists that influenced me
  • What I listen to now
  • How I got used to different genres
2

Music Is Important To Me

Music is part of my daily life. I listen to music almost every day because it changes my mood, helps me relax and reminds me of important moments.

  • Music changes my emotions and energy
  • I use music while gaming, studying or relaxing
  • Different songs remind me of different moments
  • I got used to listening to music every day
“For me, music is not only entertainment. It is also connected to memories, emotions and people.”
3

What I Used To Listen To

During the pandemic and gaming years, my music taste was very different.

  • I used to listen to gaming highlight music
  • I used to enjoy fast trap songs
  • I used to listen to montage and edit music
  • I used to follow viral TikTok songs
  • I used to play quarantine playlists every day
  • I used to prefer hype music with strong bass
4

Gaming & Internet Influence

During quarantine, I used to spend many hours watching gaming content and streamers online.

  • I used to watch gaming highlights
  • I used to watch Fortnite montages
  • I used to enjoy Call of Duty edits
  • I used to follow streamers and YouTubers
  • I used to prefer energetic music while gaming

What I Listen To Now

Today my music taste is calmer, more personal and emotional.

  • I got used to listening to calmer music
  • I got used to enjoying old school songs
  • I got used to listening to emotional music
  • I got used to different music genres
  • I got used to appreciating lyrics more

Why I Listen To Rubén Rada

I got used to listening to Rubén Rada because his music reminds me of my grandfather.

  • My grandfather used to listen to him a lot
  • I got used to hearing his songs at family moments
  • His music became emotional and nostalgic for me
  • Now those songs feel more personal
