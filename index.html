My Music Taste:
Before vs Now

This presentation is about how my music taste changed over the years, from gaming music and hype songs during quarantine to more emotional and personal music today.

  • The music I listened to before
  • The artists that influenced me
  • What I listen to now
  • How music became more emotional for me
2

Why Music Is Important To Me

Music is part of my daily life. I listen to music almost every day because it changes my mood, helps me relax and reminds me of important moments.

  • Music changes my energy and emotions
  • I use music when gaming, studying or relaxing
  • Different songs remind me of different stages of my life
  • I enjoy discovering new artists and genres
“For me, music is not only entertainment. It is also connected to memories, emotions and people.”
3

My Music Taste Before

During the pandemic and gaming years, my music taste was very different.

  • Gaming highlight music
  • Montage and edit songs
  • Fast trap music
  • Viral TikTok songs
  • Quarantine playlists
  • Hype music with strong bass
The music I listened to before gave me energy and matched the gaming videos and online content I watched every day.
4

Gaming & Internet Influence

Gaming and internet culture influenced my music taste a lot.

What I Watched

  • Gaming highlights
  • Fortnite montages
  • Call of Duty edits
  • Streamers and YouTubers

Music Style

  • Strong bass
  • Fast beats
  • Energetic drops
  • Electronic and trap sounds

6

Freestyle Battles & Trap

One of my favorite things during quarantine was watching freestyle battles and listening to trap music.

  • The rappers were very creative
  • The battles were intense and exciting
  • I liked the improvisation and audience energy
  • Trap music mixed emotions with hype beats
Freestyle battles made urban music feel more alive and entertaining for me.
7

What I Listen To Now

Today my music taste is calmer, more personal and more emotional.

  • Old school music
  • Trap and reggaeton
  • Music with emotional meaning
  • Relaxing and nostalgic songs
  • Different genres mixed together
Before, I mostly listened to music for hype and energy. Now, music also helps me remember moments and emotions.
8

Why I Listen To Rubén Rada

Rubén Rada became very important to me because his music reminds me of my grandfather.

  • My grandfather listened to him a lot
  • His music feels nostalgic and emotional
  • It reminds me of good family moments
  • Now those songs feel more personal to me
I think music becomes more special when it is connected to people we love.
